---
title: "Adding records"
description: "Step-by-step guide to contributing new vehicle cut points and supplementary installation data to the GPSpedia catalog."
---

Any authorized contributor can submit new vehicle records to the GPSpedia catalog. Submissions go through a three-stage wizard that checks for duplicates, captures cut point data, and optionally collects supplementary installation information.

## Who can add records

Record submission is available to all logged-in users who have been granted contributor access. If you do not see **Agregar Nuevo** in the side menu, your account does not yet have contributor permissions. Contact your administrator to request access.

## Opening the add-record wizard

There are two ways to reach the form:

1. Open the side menu and tap **Agregar Nuevo**.
2. Navigate directly to `add_cortes.html` in your browser.

## Submission walkthrough

The wizard has three stages. Stages 1 and 2 are required. Stage 3 is optional.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stage 1: Vehicle verification">
    Fill in the vehicle information fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Marca** | Vehicle brand (e.g., Toyota, Ford) |
    | **Modelo** | Model name (e.g., Hilux, Ranger) |
    | **Año Desde** | First production year this record applies to |
    | **Tipo de Encendido** | Ignition type (conventional, push-button, etc.) |
    | **Categoría** | Vehicle category (car, truck, motorcycle, etc.) |
    | **Versiones Aplicables** | Equipment trims or versions this cut applies to |

    As you type, the form queries the catalog for phonetically similar entries using fuzzy matching (Levenshtein distance). If the system detects a likely spelling variant, it displays a suggestion below the field:

    > *Quizás quisiste decir: Toyota*

    Tap the suggestion to apply it. This helps keep brand and model names consistent across the catalog.

    Once you complete the fields, tap **Verificar** to check whether a matching vehicle already exists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stage 1.5: Match review">
    If the verification finds existing records that are similar to the vehicle you entered, a list of matches is shown. You must decide how to proceed:

    <Columns cols={3}>
      <Card title="Add cut to existing" icon="plus">
        Attach your new cut point to an existing vehicle record. Use this when the vehicle already exists but is missing this particular cut.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Add supplementary info" icon="info">
        Add supporting data (cable feeds, aperture notes, important warnings) to an existing record without creating a new cut entry.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Create new" icon="file-plus">
        Start a brand-new vehicle record. Use this only when you are certain the vehicle is not already in the catalog.
      </Card>
    </Columns>

    If no matches are found, the wizard advances automatically to Stage 2.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stage 2: Cut point data">
    Enter the details for the cut point. The following fields are required:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Tipo de Corte 1** | Type of cut (power, ground, ignition, etc.) |
    | **Configuración Relay 1** | Relay wiring configuration for this cut |
    | **Ubicación del Corte 1** | Physical location of the cut on the vehicle |
    | **Color de Cable del Corte 1** | Wire color at the cut point |
    | **Imagen del Corte 1** | Photo of the cut location (required) |

    If you are creating a new vehicle record (not adding to an existing one), you must also upload an **Imagen del Vehículo** — a representative photo of the vehicle.

    Tap **Guardar Corte** when all required fields are complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stage 3: Supplementary info (optional)">
    After saving the cut point, you can attach additional installation data to the record. All sections in this stage are optional.

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Apertura">
        Notes or diagrams related to opening or disassembling the vehicle to reach the cut location.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Cable de Alimentación">
        Information about the power feed cable, including location, color, and gauge.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Nota Importante">
        Any critical warnings or caveats that other installers should know before working on this vehicle.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

    Each section also accepts supporting images. Tap **Finalizar** when done, or skip the entire stage by tapping the skip link. Either action returns you to the home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Anti-duplication check

The `checkVehicle` function compares your Stage 1 input against every existing record in the catalog. It matches on brand, model, year range, ignition type, and equipment version. When it finds a close match, it surfaces those records in Stage 1.5 so you can attach your contribution to an existing entry instead of creating a duplicate.

<Warning>
  Do not create a new vehicle record if a matching entry already exists. Use the **Add cut to existing** option in Stage 1.5 to contribute your cut point to the correct record. Duplicate entries reduce catalog quality and make it harder for other installers to find reliable data.
</Warning>

## Contributor attribution

<Note>
  The **Colaborador** field is populated automatically from your session name. You do not need to enter your name manually. Make sure you are signed in with your correct account before submitting.
</Note>
