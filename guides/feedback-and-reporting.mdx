title: "Feedback and reporting"
description: "How to vote on cut point quality, report incorrect data, and contact the GPSpedia team."
---

GPSpedia includes two per-cut feedback mechanisms that help surface reliable data and flag incorrect records. These controls appear as overlay buttons on each cut point image.

## Marking a cut as useful

If a cut point record is accurate and helped you complete an installation, tap the **👍 Marcar como útil** button overlaid on the cut image.

- The button turns green immediately, giving you instant visual confirmation that your vote was recorded (optimistic UI).
- Your vote is persisted for the current session. You can only vote once per cut point per session — the button becomes inactive after you vote.
- The total number of useful votes determines which cut point is labeled **Recommended** when a vehicle has multiple cut records for the same ignition type. The cut with the most votes is promoted to the top and marked as the recommended option.

## Reporting a problem

If a cut point contains incorrect data — wrong wire color, incorrect location, a bad image, or any other error — tap the **⚠️ Reportar un problema** button on the cut image.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the report button">
    Tap **⚠️ Reportar un problema** on the cut image you want to report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the problem">
    A prompt appears asking: *"Describe el problema con este corte:"*

    Enter a clear description of what is wrong. For example: *"El color del cable está incorrecto, debería ser rojo con franja negra"*.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **OK** to send the report. If you tap **Cancel** or leave the field empty, no report is submitted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Problem reports are sent directly to the admin **Inbox**. An administrator will review the report and update or remove the affected cut record. You will not receive an automated reply, but your report is visible to the team immediately.
</Note>

## Suggesting a year range

If you believe a vehicle record applies to a year range that is not currently listed, you can submit a year suggestion using the **suggestYear** action available within the detail modal. This sends a request to the admin team to extend or adjust the applicable year range for that record.

## Contacting the team

For general feedback, questions, or issues that are not tied to a specific cut record, use the contact form:

1. Scroll to the footer of any page.
2. Tap **Contáctanos**.
3. Fill in the form and submit.

The contact form is suitable for feature requests, account issues, and general platform feedback.
