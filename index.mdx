## What is GPSpedia?

GPSpedia is a specialized technical catalog built for installers, technicians, and professionals who work with GPS tracking and vehicle security systems. Instead of searching through scattered sources, GPSpedia gives you standardized, structured information exactly where you need it — in the field.

<Info>
  GPSpedia is a read-only catalog for most users. Data entry is available to authorized contributors through the **Agregar Nuevo** form.
</Info>

## Who uses GPSpedia?

GPSpedia is built for professionals in the vehicle security and GPS tracking industry:

- **GPS installers** who need quick wiring references on the job
- **Workshop technicians** verifying cut points and relay configurations
- **Team supervisors** managing installer teams and reviewing activity
- **Equipment distributors** supporting their clients with technical documentation

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request access">
    Contact the GPSpedia team to receive your account credentials. See [Accessing the Platform](/accessing-the-platform) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Open GPSpedia and enter your username and password. Your session is stored securely in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a vehicle">
    Use the search bar to look up a brand, model, or year. Or browse by category and brand using the catalog navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the cut point">
    Select the vehicle entry to see the recommended cut point, cable color, location, and relay configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
