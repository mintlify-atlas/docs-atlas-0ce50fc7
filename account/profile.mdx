---
title: "Your profile"
description: "View and update your display name and password in GPSpedia."
---

Your profile page lets you manage your personal account information. You can update your display name and change your password at any time. Your username, however, is permanent and cannot be changed after account creation.

## Navigate to your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the side menu">
    Click the menu icon to expand the side navigation panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Mi Perfil / Usuarios">
    Click **Mi Perfil / Usuarios** in the side menu. This opens the users management page (`users.html`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate your account row">
    Find your account in the user table. Your current display name and role are shown in your row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editable fields

The following fields can be updated from your profile:

| Field | Label in app | Editable |
|---|---|---|
| Display name | Nombre Completo | Yes |
| Password | Contraseña | Yes |
| Username | Nombre de Usuario | No |
| Role | Rol | No (set by Jefe) |

### Updating your display name

Your display name (Nombre Completo) is the human-readable name shown throughout the platform. To update it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Mi Perfil / Usuarios">
    Navigate to the profile page using the side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your record">
    Click the edit action on your account row to open the edit form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Nombre Completo">
    Clear the **Nombre Completo** field and type your new display name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Guardar** (Save) to apply the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your display name (Nombre Completo) appears in the activity dashboard logs and is credited on cut point entries you contribute. Keep it accurate and professional.
</Note>

### Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Mi Perfil / Usuarios">
    Navigate to the profile page using the side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the password change form">
    Click the change password action on your account row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Type your new password in the **Nueva Contraseña** field. Choose a strong, unique password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and save">
    Confirm the new password and click **Guardar** to apply the change. You will need to use the new password on your next login.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you cannot change

<Warning>
  Your username (Nombre de Usuario) is permanent. It is set at the time your account is created and cannot be modified afterward — not even by a Jefe. If your username needs to change, a Jefe must delete your account and create a new one.
</Warning>

Your role is also not self-editable. Role changes are made by a Jefe during account creation or through an admin update.
